White china platters, wooden boards and baskets are available for food display on a borrow, wash, and return basis. All borrowed items are to be returned to Megunticook Market, washed and dried, within 2 days post event. If you would prefer Megunticook Market to pick up these items, please schedule in advance. Small disposable plates, utensils and napkins are available on request. Additional paper and disposable tableware are available and charges may apply.
