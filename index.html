About Us

Your Premier Destination for Gourmet Delights!

Megunticook Market is a charming country store with specialty food, catering, baked goods, prepared food, fine wine and cheese.

Camden’s Country Market. A full line of outstanding grocery items, custom-cut meat, wine, cheese, and baked goods. Great selection of Maine-made specialty food, wine, and farm-fresh produce. Our Story

Services

Culinary Delights at Megunticook Market, Unveiling a world of flavor isn't just a catchy phrase at Megunticook Market, it's our promise. We offer a variety of services to tantalize your taste buds and make every occasion truly special.

Welcome to Megunticook Market, your premier destination for gourmet delights! Nestled in the heart of Camden, our charming country store offers a delightful array of specialty food, catering services, baked goods, fine wine, and artisanal cheeses. At Megunticook Market, we pride ourselves on providing the best selection in Camden, with over 250 international and domestic wines, local microbrews, and a bounty of Maine-made specialty foods and farm-fresh produce.

Market

Indulge in a culinary adventure at our market. Explore a treasure trove of fresh, local produce, custom-cut prime meats, and an extensive selection of specialty foods. We boast the best selection in Camden of domestic and international wines, alongside enticing local microbrews. Don't forget to grab a slice of our legendary pizza, handcrafted sandwiches, or fresh-baked goods for a delightful treat on the go.

  • Wide variety of fresh, local produce and custom-cut meats.
  • Over 250 domestic and international wines, plus local microbrews.
  • Freshly prepared meals, pizzas, sandwiches & baked goods.
